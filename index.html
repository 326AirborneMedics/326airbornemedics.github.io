Who We Are

The 326th Airborne Medical Company (Reenacted) is a World War II living history and reenactment unit dedicated to portraying the medical personnel who served with America’s airborne forces in the European Theater.

Through living history displays, public events, educational programs, and historically inspired field impressions, we work to keep the story of the airborne medic alive for future generations.
