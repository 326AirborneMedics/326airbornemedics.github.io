Join Today

The 326th Airborne Medical Company (Reenacted) welcomes individuals who share an interest in World War II history, living history, and honoring the service of America's airborne medical personnel.

Who Can Join?

We welcome people from all backgrounds and experience levels. Whether you are an experienced reenactor or completely new to the hobby, we are happy to help you learn and grow within the unit.

What We Do

  • Living history displays
  • Public education events
  • Historical commemorations
  • World War II reenactments
  • Airborne medical impressions

Required Qualities

  • Respect for history
  • Professional conduct
  • Willingness to learn
  • Commitment to authenticity
  • Positive attitude and teamwork

Uniforms and Equipment

New members are not expected to purchase a complete impression immediately. The unit can provide guidance on uniforms, equipment, approved vendors, and recommended purchases as members become involved.
