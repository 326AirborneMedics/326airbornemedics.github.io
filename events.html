Events

Living history displays, educational programs, commemorations, and World War II reenactments honoring the legacy of the 326th Airborne Medical Company.

The 326th Airborne Medical Company (Reenacted) participates in a variety of living history events, commemorations, educational programs, and World War II reenactments throughout the year.

Types of Events We Attend

  • World War II reenactments
  • Living history displays
  • Military vehicle shows
  • Museum events
  • Veterans commemorations
  • Educational programs
  • Public history demonstrations

What We Display

Depending on the event, our displays may include airborne medical equipment, aid station displays, litters, field medical demonstrations, and other items representing the mission of the wartime airborne medic.
